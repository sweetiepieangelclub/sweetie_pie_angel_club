Meet Mina

Mina (she/her), founder of Sweetie Pie Angel Club, is an Iranian-American Breathwork Facilitator, Reiki Master, artist, emotional influencer, and vibe curator based between Bushwick and the online sphere. A Los Angeles native turned Brooklyn creative, she's been facilitating healing spaces since 2016. She formerly taught Kundalini Yoga before recognizing its cult dynamics, an experience that deepened her commitment to integrity, trauma-informed care, and authenticity in spiritual spaces.

Mina's work is queer-inclusive, collaborative, and playful. She sees clients privately (online and in Bushwick), teaches Reiki-infused Breathwork, and leads Reiki trainings. Sweetie Pie Angel Club isn't just about healing, but also about expression and connection, with upcoming workshops like breathwork into intuitive painting and other cross-disciplinary collaborations with local artists. Mina curates breathwork loft parties with her partner, as well as collaborates with Maha Rose, Reforesters Laboratory, and The Meadow Dumbo. She has been featured by global wellness collectives like DAYBREAKER, and has produced "Conscious Clubbing" events at Brooklyn staples like Signal, Earthly Delights, and Mood Ring.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your first session.

Reiki is a gentle, non-invasive energy healing practice that works with your body's natural energy field to promote relaxation and balance. During a session with Mina, you'll lie fully clothed on a table while she uses light touch or hovering hands to channel healing energy. Most clients feel deeply relaxed, some experience warmth or tingling, and many leave feeling lighter and more at peace.

Breathwork is a guided breathing practice that uses intentional breath patterns to access deeper states of relaxation, emotional release, and clarity. Mina's sessions are trauma-informed and held in a safe, supportive container. If you have any health conditions including cardiovascular issues, epilepsy, or are pregnant, please reach out before booking so we can discuss what's right for you.
